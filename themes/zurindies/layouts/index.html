Welcome to zürindies!

We're a community of developers, artists, musicians, and enthusiasts in and around Zürich, who meet up every Saturday for casual co-working sessions. Join us to:

  • Make progress on your project (or start a new one!)
  • Find inspiration and exchange ideas
  • Get playtesting feedback

We usually meet at St. Jakob Beck & Kafi Viadukt throughout the day. All experience levels are welcome, just bring something you can work on. More details can be found on our discord.

Note: If you are looking for a job or hiring, please use other venues such as the SGDA.
