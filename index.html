What is zürindies?

We’re a group of local game developers in Zürich, meeting up for casual co-working on Saturdays. Come and find people to play-test your work in progress, or just have a chat!

We normally meet at Café Auer from 1pm onwards, but check @zurindies for the latest. To find us, look for the gamepad on the table.
